Then there are the people buying a gift for a partner, a parent, or a kid who would not stop talking about their trip to Yellowstone for three years—those are the pack people. 

I have bought gifts for both types, and I can tell you that picking the right item matters. ### Are lone wolves actually a real thing in nature?

No, the idea of a "lone wolf" as a cool, permanent outsider is mostly a myth. In the wild, a lone wolf is usually just a young dispersing male trying to find a mate and establish a new territory. It is a temporary life stage, not a personality trait. 

Wild wolves are intensely social animals that live in family packs led by the breeding parents. If you want to learn more about actual wolf behavior and biology, we cover it extensively on our main [wolf animal page](/animals/wolf/).
