### What hummingbird items should I avoid buying?

You should never buy red-dyed hummingbird nectar or feeders with plastic bee guards. Red dye is unnecessary and potentially harmful to the birds, while plastic bee guards often trap and slowly kill native pollinators. 

Instead of buying questionable feeder supplies, stick to apparel, drinkware, or native seed packets.
