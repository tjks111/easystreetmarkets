### What do sea turtles symbolize?

Across dozens of cultures, sea turtles symbolize longevity, patience, resilience, and the ability to find your way home. Because they can live for up to 80 years and navigate thousands of miles of open ocean to return to their birthplace, they are universally viewed as quiet, enduring survivors.

### What is the "World Turtle" myth?

The World Turtle is a mythological concept where a giant turtle carries the earth on its back. This image appears independently in Hindu, Native American (Haudenosaunee and Lenape), and Chinese traditions. It represents the idea that the world is supported by something ancient, stable, and quietly powerful.
